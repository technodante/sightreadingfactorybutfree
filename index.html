Sight Reading Factory, But Free!

Have you ever wanted to use www.sightreadingfactory.com, but found that it was too pricy and/or inconvenient?

What if you wanted it integrated into the notation software you already use and love?

Introducing "Sight Reading Factory, But Free!"

Simply download this file and put it in your plugins folder for musescore.

If you have any trouble, follow this tutorial for help.

Once installed, open it from the plugins tab and adjust the settings to your liking.

Once complete, an empty score will generate. Then press the m key to see your score!
